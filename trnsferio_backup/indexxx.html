Frequently asked

Security, coverage, and getting started.

How secure is FluxPay?

We follow a defense-in-depth model: network isolation, HSM-backed key management, tokenization, TLS 1.3, and continuous monitoring. Independent audits: SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, PCI-DSS for card data.

Where are payouts available?

Local rails in 30+ countries and 72 settlement currencies. If we can’t pay locally, we SWIFT it.

Can I migrate from another provider?

Yes—bulk import for customers, vaulted cards, webhooks, and ledger continuity to maintain reporting history.

Do you support SSO and roles?

Yes, SAML SSO and SCIM for provisioning. Roles down to transaction-level permissions and full audit trails.
